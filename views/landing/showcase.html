Museum of Science and Industry, 5700 S. Lake Shore Dr., Chicago
Free admission
Badges and raffle prizes open to all youth attendees 1,000 attendees expected

This summer thousands of Chicago youth have been exploring the city through programs offered by more than 100 community organizations through the Chicago Summer of Learning. As a result, Chicago’s youth have enjoyed a variety of opportunities to improve their skills and learn new ones in topics like sports and reading, archaeology and architecture, poetry, film making, fashion design and more – all while earning digital badges and leveling up to new challenges!

The Chicago Summer of Learning Summer Showcase brings all these organizations under one roof to showcase their work, provide an opportunity to learn new skills, and continue inspiring others. There are three components to the events:

  1. A showcase for organizations to display their summer learning and projects.
  2. A Maker Party space where youth can make or learn new skills.
  3. A youth talent showcase where teams will show off their performance abilities.
