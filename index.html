Learning happens everywhere in the city—in libraries, parks, museums and cultural institutions, community-based organizations, colleges and universities, schools, and beyond. School stops for the summer, but learning never should.

More than 100 organizations across the city have joined forces to integrate learning into summer activities for youth of all ages in every Chicago neighborhood.

With your help, we'll launch the largest citywide summer learning campaign in the nation.

Participants learn over the summer with local programs (i.e.: zoo, library, parks) and earn badges along the way.

The badges they earn roll up into city-level badges.

The city-level badges unlock access to the challenges where learners can make projects and learn more skills, and then showcase their work at a set of events in Chicago at the end of the summer.

All of that badge info—everything from the initial organization badges to the top-level challenges and badges—feeds back into the schools in the fall.

Chicago Summer of Learning and Badges

Recognizing summer learning for youth and connecting them to additional opportunities.

Let's go through some of the basics about Chicago Summer of Learning badges and earn your first badge in the process.

Start

Badges as recognition

Badges are digital representations of skills and achievements. They are evidence-based so that each badge carries the information needed to understand and vet that badge. We can use badges to recognize and capture learning milestones and skill development, connect learners to additional learning opportunities, and then signal all of that learning to stakeholders like schools or employers.

Next

Learning is happening all over Chicago

But it's difficult to get recognition for all of that learning and to connect it back to schools or job opportunities. Badges give us a way to recognize all of the learning - whether its at a park, museum, library or online course - and give learners digital credentials that communicate the skills that they have developed.

Next

Unlocking Opportunity

The badges in the Chicago Summer of Learning will recognize learning across all of the summer programs. The badges the youth earn will unlock additional learning opportunities and challenges. All of the badge information can be carried back to schools and job fairs in the fall.
